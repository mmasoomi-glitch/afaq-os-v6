AFAQ ATTENDANCE SYSTEM - USER GUIDE

MANDATORY READING FOR ALL AFAQ ALNASEEM STAFF. This document is required before your first shift. Non-compliance affects your KPI.

1. WHAT IS THE AFAQ ATTENDANCE SYSTEM?

The Afaq Attendance System is a digital clocking tool that runs on the office computer and is accessible by every staff member using their phone or any device connected to the office WiFi. There is nothing to install. You simply open a link in your browser.

  • Records your arrival and departure every shift with your name and exact timestamp.
  • Works on any phone or PC on the office WiFi.
  • Saves all data automatically.

2. HOW TO ACCESS THE SYSTEM

  1. Connect to Office WiFi: Make sure your device is on the office network.
  2. Open Your Browser: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, etc.
  3. Type The Link: Your manager will provide the link, which looks like http://192.168.X.X:3456.

3. SHIFT SCHEDULE

Your shift times are displayed on the attendance page. The buttons to clock in and out will become active only within a 15-minute window around your scheduled time.

4. HOW TO CLOCK IN & OUT

  1. Open the attendance link on your device.
  2. Find your name on the dashboard.
  3. Wait for the button for your current shift event (e.g., "Morning In") to become active.
  4. Press the button once.
  5. Look for the confirmation message.

5. TROUBLESHOOTING

  • Page won't open: Check that you are connected to the office WiFi.
  • Button is not active: You are likely outside the 15-minute clock-in window. Please wait.
  • Page is not loading: Inform your manager to ensure the server application is running.
